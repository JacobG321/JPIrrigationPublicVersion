Irrigation System Installations

Our Process

J&P Irrigation uses irrigreen technology. Checkout the benefits of the irrigreen system in the video above!

Irrigation installation begins with an easy and free estimate from our irrigation system expert. We will meet with you to discuss the watering necessities for your landscape. Our installation expert will look at every feature of your property such as sunlight, shade, drainage, ground elevation, water pressure, and electrical outputs to make sure that all facets are considered during the design stage. Our installation expert will also discuss your current and future plans for changes to the landscape to ensure your lawn is as malleable as possible for your needs.

After working with you to figure out a blueprint and pricing, our team will setup a schedule to begin work. Our experienced installer will use state of the art equipment to ensure we leave little to to no evidence behind!

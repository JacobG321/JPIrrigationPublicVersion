Lawn Aeration

What is aeration?

We use a machine that will pull small plugs of dirt and grass throughout your yard. This process breaks up and loosens the soil. Aeration will allow more air and nutrients into the root zone. With better nourishment, the roots will be able to spread, grow deeper, and absorb more fertilizer. This leads to a healthier, greener, happier yard.

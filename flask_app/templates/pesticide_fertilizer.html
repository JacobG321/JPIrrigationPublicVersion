Pesticides and Fertilizer Applications

Fertilizer Application

Fertilizer applications are crucial for plant growth, development, and overall health. We can set up a schedule custom to your lawns needs. Based on what type of grass, how much you water, and sunlight exposure we will design a custom plan suitable for all factors. We offer the "Big 3", nitrogen (Plant growth), phosphorus (root health and root development), and potassium (overall plant health). We also offer micronutrients and bio-stimulates needed for development such as iron, magnesium, manganese, sea kelp, and seaweed.

Pesticide Application

Pesticides is a general term used for any unwanted "pests", insects, weeds, and fungus. The best way to get rid of them is to prevent them before they start. We can offer a plan to spray for many unsightly broadleaf weeds that move to take over your yard like a wild fire. Just like weeds, fungus can also spread rampid if not controlled and protected against. When the weather gets hot, and the soil is damp, it becomes a breeding ground for fungus to destroy all of your hard work and beautiful grass. Insects are obnoxious and can be harmful if not controlled. Above the ground we can rid your lawn of ants, ticks, fleas, and many others. Under the surface we help block grubs, and similar insects from burrowing in the root level and eating the healthy roots of your grass. If not controlled they will leave brown patches and dead spots in your lawn as if you set up a big magnifying glass and burned a hole into your turf.
